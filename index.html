A

Strategic Objectives

2027 & Beyond
01
High Priority

Market Presence in Jeddah

Simultaneous launch of both concepts in 2027, establishing a dual-brand footprint in the Western Region's primary market.

02
High Priority

Multi-Segment Targeting

Sobhy Kaber (Fine Dining) targets the premium evening segment; La Venue (All-Day) captures breakfast-through-dinner across wider demographics.

03
High Priority

Achieve ROI ≤ 10 Years

Daily cover target of ≥ 880 combined orders across 330 operating days to ensure the base-case payback timeline is met.

04
Medium Priority

Customer Loyalty & Retention

Loyalty programmes, terrace experience, and delivery partnerships (Jahez, HungerStation) to maximise repeat customer rate.

05
Medium Priority

Operational Excellence

Unified quality benchmarks and a centralised training team to protect brand integrity and reduce operational variance.

06
Future Phase

Regional Expansion

Makkah, Madinah, and Taif within 3 years. Feasibility study commences Q4 2027 once both Jeddah branches reach steady-state.

B

Risk Register

Key Risks & Mitigations

Rising Operating Costs

Medium

Weekly food-cost tracking tied to POS; fixed-price supplier contracts locked before launch to hedge against input cost inflation.

Low Occupancy Rate

High

Structured soft opening followed by a full marketing launch. 6-month occupancy KPI reviewed weekly by the Operations Director.

Construction & Fit-Out Delays

Medium

Strict milestone schedule with contractual penalty clauses. Bi-weekly site progress reviews with buffer built into pre-opening timeline.

Competitive Pressure in Jeddah

Low

Clear brand differentiation through unique concepts, quality consistency, and the complete dining experience.

C

Strategic Recommendations

1

Drive Full Occupancy in First 6 Months

Target corporate events, large Jeddah families, and social media influencers. Occupancy in this period is the single largest lever for closing the payback gap.

2

Monitor Food Cost Daily

POS linked to inventory management. Weekly food-cost report issued every Sunday to the CEO. The 30% target is a ceiling, not a guideline.

3

Delivery as an Additional Revenue Stream

Pre-launch agreements with Jahez and HungerStation. Delivery accounts for 130 of the 880 combined daily covers — a material revenue contributor.

4

Loyalty Programme from Day One

Points system, Ramadan promotions, and birthday rewards operational at launch. Early loyalty sign-ups are a tracked KPI in the first 90 days.

5

Monthly Financial Reporting to the Board

KPI dashboard vs. budget submitted by the 10th of each month. Variance exceeding ±10% on any major cost line triggers an immediate review meeting.

6

Prepare for Makkah & Madinah Expansion

Feasibility study commences Q4 2027. Jeddah serves as the operational blueprint for all subsequent Western Region branches.
